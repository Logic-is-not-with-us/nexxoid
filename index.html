FAQ

How fast can you ship?
A small site typically ships in 7–14 days once content is ready. Premium builds and integrations take longer.
Do you handle Google Business & local SEO?
Yes—setup/cleanup, NAP consistency, category tuning, and site structure that supports local search.
Can you integrate bookings, payments, or CRM?
Yes. We can connect calendars, forms, Stripe, email flows, and lightweight CRM pipelines depending on your needs.
What do you need from me?
Logo/brand (or we can polish it), services/pricing, photos, and Google Business access. We’ll guide you.
